About Us

The Power of Kamathgiri Steels

What started out as a journey towards becoming the best steel manufacturing unit in the country, turned out to be Kamathigiri Group, a well-established unit that has further ventured into the Medium and Heacy Structural Steels, Flats and Rounds and Squares segment with fully Automated Plant at APIIC, Atchutapuram, Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, all this in just a year.

The installed capacity of the plant is 1.4 Million tonnes per annum producing Medium and Heavy Structural Steel Products, Flats and Rounds, Squares of different grades like E-250, E-350, EN8/8D etc., This manufacturing facility also supports nearly 250 people with direct employment and around 500 people with indirect employment.

The newly setup Steel Rolling Mill has commenced to cater to the needs of the growing demand in steel and especially in Structural Steel items such as Angles, Channels, Beams, RSJ in various sizes and grades. These Structural Steels are an integral part of infrastructure and construction industries as they give shape and strength to the buildings, bridges, transmission towers, industrial sheds and many other applications. Kamathgiri Steels guarantees meticulous quality, transparent business association and competitive price.

This unit will roll the high quality Structural, Flats and Rounds, and Square items from the best quality raw materials. These Steel products(items) are for a unique choice for general pupose structures, Bridges and Flyovers, Dams, Thermal and Power Plants, Airport infrastructures, Engineering Structurers, Engineering and Automobile Industries and so on.

Our Promoters

Sri Satyanarayan Agarwal, Chairman and Sri Manish Agarwal, Managing Director of United Group, Chennai, incepted the organization in 1975, successfully developed and attained a respected leadership role in dealing with Carbon, Alloy and Special Steels and also developed Bright Bar Manufacturing Unit, in the name of Mahalakshmi Bright Steel Industries Pvt Ltd, one of the largest units in India, manufacturing 3000 Metric Tonnes per month. This dynamic group, with its rich knowledge, experience and wisdom in domestic steel sector decided to establish a Stell Rolling Mill(MSME) in the name of Kamathgiri Steels Pvt Ltd(KSPL) at Visakhapatnam, in the year 2020 with production capacity of 12000 Metric Tonnes per month, manufacturing large structural steels, Medium & Heavy Angles, Channels, Beams and rounds with initial installed capacity of 1,50,000 Metric Tonnes per annum. The commercial pproduction commenced in January, 2022.
